A Designer solves different Problems

Graphic Design also known as communication design, is the art and practice of planning and projecting ideas and experiences with visualand textual content.

A Designer creates novel Forms

Successful design balances things—  forms, terms, texts — and modernity , then delight the users. The form it takes can be physical or virtual and can include images, words even emotions.

A Designer understands Goals

Designers need not be a specialist in everything to fulfill their role. The designer must receive the most important information from specialists. Any design task he undertake must serve a goal.
